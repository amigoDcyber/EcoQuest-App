EcoQuest 🌱

EcoQuest is a mobile app that helps users learn about sustainability and protecting the environment. The app provides simple educational content about topics such as recycling, renewable energy, and reducing pollution.

Users can explore sustainability lessons, watch short educational videos, and test their knowledge through a fun 10-question quiz. After completing the quiz, the app displays the user’s score and allows them to track their progress.

The goal of EcoQuest is to make learning about sustainability interactive, engaging, and accessible, encouraging people to adopt environmentally friendly habits in their daily lives.

Features

🌍 Learn about sustainability topics

🎥 Educational videos

🧠 10-question quiz game

📊 Score tracking

📱 Simple and user-friendly interface
